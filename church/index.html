Victory Presby Church

“Go into all the world and preach the gospel to all creation” (Mark 16:15, NASB). “Go therefore and make disciples of all the nations … teaching them to observe all things that I have commanded you; and lo, I am with you always, even to the end of the age” (Matthew 28:19-20) Jesus Christ gave His Church—this body of spiritually transformed believers—a responsibility to carry out. The Church’s mission is to preach the gospel of the Kingdom of God and make disciples throughout the world, teaching them exactly what Jesus taught.

As a church, all our activities seek to fulfill this core mandate given to us by our lord Jesus Christ. When this mandate is fulfilled, the promise Jesus gave us in “Matthew 16:18 , And I tell you that you are Peter, and on this rock I will build My church, and the gates of Hades will not prevail against it”, will materialize.

Join us as we build a church for God.
